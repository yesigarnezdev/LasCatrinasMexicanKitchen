Milwaukee, WI

La Chinampa Mexican Food brings the vibrant, authentic flavors of Mexico straight to the heart of Milwaukee. With two beloved locations serving the community, we've become a cornerstone of Milwaukee's culinary landscape.

Our recipes are inspired by generations of tradition — from slow-cooked meats to hand-pressed tortillas, every dish tells a story of love, culture, and home. Whether you visit us on 2nd Street or National Ave, you'll always find a warm welcome and an even warmer plate.
